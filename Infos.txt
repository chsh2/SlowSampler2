SlowSampler2 is a VST3 plugin to play audio samples, focusing on providing rich options of time stretching and pitch tuning, in order to convert different types of sounds into musical instruments.

---

The library is built with a modified version of Camomile and several external objects:

* libpd 0.52.0-test1
* Camomile v1.0.8-beta10
* pd-else v1.0-beta44
* cyclone v0.6.0

Please refer to the following repository for more information:
https://github.com/chsh2/Camomile
